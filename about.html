About me

Cook by day, coder by night. not even Bruce Wayne dared to do this much. My name is Marizabelle Aldana, I'm a 27 years old, devoted cat lover, utterly obsessed with my 20+ rescued kitty family. Based in Guatemala, currently living in London, in the hunt for university and hoping to come back and start my studies in Game Arts and Design.
My qualifications jump from a field to another, after accomplishing all my High Education studies, I coursed 1 year of Software Engineering and 1 year of Communication Science and Advertising at university San Carlos de Guatemala. Acquired Pâtissier 1 year qualification at Escuela Superior Culinaria Las Margaritas.
Since December last year (2021) I had the great chance to enroll Shecodes workshops thanks to a honestly, life changing programme they offer, so I was one of the fortunate girls who were awarded with a full Scholarship for all their courses. I feel truly happy and grateful of have been granted with this awesome opportunity who rocketed me back to coding, thank you Matt Delac.
So far, I’ve acquired the following diplomas:

  • Shecodes Basics
  • Shecodes Plus
  • Shecodes Responsive
The early years in my life were alike many other 90's kids, spent their younghood, making great friends and spending those afternoons playing with your that time most reliable pal, the Nintendo 64, which of course came after spending the earliest of my childhood with the Super Nintendo Entertainment system or just commonly called SNES, and kept collecting videogame consoles since then.
When I grew up older, I naturally developed a deep interest for technology and, besides being a hardcore gamer, I started gaining some programming skills on my own. Years after I’m making a comeback to the software and front-end development world as well as I also plan to keep polishing my culinary arts skills for days to come.
